Security telegram bot

Security telegram bot

This bot has access to the computer's webcam and stores two photos: your photo and a "banned" photo. Whenever the script fails to recognize you in front of the screen, it sends a photo of the person attempting to use your computer along with the timestamp to a Telegram channel. Additionally, this bot can display logs of any attempts to use your computer. This bot can:

  1. Show intrusctions of usage
  2. Start and stop security system
  3. Show security logs
